About Us

Agape Kids Empowerment Ability Network is a community-based nonprofit located in Kamuli District, Uganda. We support children with disabilities and their families through inclusive programs, assistive technology, therapy, education, and economic empowerment. Our work is grounded in compassion, inclusion, and dignity for all.

Our Programs

  • Therapy & Rehabilitation Services
  • Assistive Devices Support (e.g., crutches, hearing aids, glasses)
  • Family & Community Empowerment Projects
  • Inclusive Education Support (school fees, supplies)
  • Livelihood Initiatives (e.g., poultry projects for parents)
